# Body Odyssey Website

## Project Overview
One-page portfolio site for Camille Dawson's creative work. Primary audience: venues, funders, collaborators.

## Projects to Feature
- **Body Odyssey** - Immersive family musical (ages 5-12), inside the human body, inflatable organs
- **The War Inside** - Award-winning adult show, hidden illness theme
- **Installation** - Giant inflatable organs (heart, liver, neuron)
- **Audio Project** - Original music (media pending)
- **Workshops** - Bespoke offerings
- **Kids Book** - Coming soon

## Assets
- `media/images/` - carrot.jpg, good-kids-pic.jpg, installation.jpg, war-inside.jpg
- `media/music/` - 7 WAV tracks (instrumentals + demos)
- `pack.pdf` - Production pack with additional images and details

## Key Context
- Creator: Camille Dawson (writer, performer, director)
- Personal connection: diagnosed with autoimmune illness as teenager
- Premiered at Southbank Centre (sold out)
- Press: 5-star reviews, praised by The Lancet
